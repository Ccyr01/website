Cooking

For the past ten years I have worked in several different kitchens. My interests in cooking started with my family, because they are all great cooks. My grandpa was a cook his entire career in the Army. My aunt has her own catering company. She cooks the food in her home kitchen and delivers to several different organizations. I started small at wingstop in 2010 then I began to explore different styles of cooking. I have since worked with seafood, tex-mex, mexican, and barbecue. Even to this day if I have some time I will experiment with my dad's smoker or just cook up a recipe I found off of the internet. Either way I have fun cooking, and I plan to do this for life.
